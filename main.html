     Our Story

    I and Rasika were introduced to each other by common friends, Mr.& Mrs. Limaye (Satish Gore was the catalyst).

    We spoke with each other for about a month on the phone and when it seemed right, decided to meet up in Navi Mumbai at Rasika's brother's place. From there on, our phone bills shot up and we realized that the only way to cut down on those was to come under one roof.

    We let our parents know. Me and my folks then drove to Pune to meet her parents. Within a week they came to Mumbai where we 'sort of' proposed to each other and agreed to go ahead and tie the knot. Our parents then dived into the scheduling and logistics while we started to search for better mobile plans to keep costs at minimum :P

    Did we propose? Hell yeah... and We had, not one, but a series of proposals. The first one was extremely formal and happened when we went out while her parents were in Mumbai. Until then, we asked questions like " What have you guys thought or decided about this alliance?" But I broke the formality and asked "Why are we being so formal? When somewhere deep down there.. we want to go ahead no matter what anyone else thinks...?" She simply smiled.... that was my clue 'haseeee to phaseeee bawaa' (If she smiles, she likes..!)

    Second was after I returned from a Pune visit. We had a great time. For once I saw a brighter and lighter side of the city. After coming back I was back to routine and so was she. But clearly my mind was not focussed. So she asked me what's the deal and the second formal proposal took place over the wireless channel...

    The third and the traditional one happened during our engagement, when I went down on one knee and proposed her with a ring in my hands. She again smiled but this time she also said "Of course... is that even a question???" and the deal was finally sealed! Well right now, we are shuttling between Mumbai-Pune and in the meanwhile trying to get all the logistics and planning done before the wedding.
